[b]Important:[/b]
- Only RimWorld 1.6 is supported.
- Older version folders are kept for historical reference only.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/MClTdVy.gif[/img]

Adds Demigryph war-mounts and their related equipment.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/wNNiH6Q.png[/img]

A powerful and majestic creature, the Demigryph was rumoured to be genetically created by RimWorld's richest imperial nobles. Eagle and lion spliced together into an apex predator, they are sought as mounts and trophies while tribes either worship them or fear them.

[b]Current features[/b]
- 9 demigryph variants
- Demigryph eggs and breeding
- Crossbreeding between all demigryph variants
- Demigryph leather, feathers, and claws
- Human demigryph-themed helms
- Demigryph banners
- Demigryph shields
- Demigryph animal armor

[b]Compatibility[/b]
- Works with Giddy Up Forked if you want to ride them:
[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3246108162]https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3246108162[/url]
- Works with Animal Apparel: Framework for animal armor:
[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3513825850]https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3513825850[/url]
- Works with Animal Gear for animal armor as an alternative compatibility path:
[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1541438907]https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1541438907[/url]
- Vanilla Expanded Framework is required for banners and shields.
- Combat Extended includes its own built-in compatibility patch for this mod.

[b]Notes[/b]
- Animal Apparel: Framework and Animal Gear are supported as alternatives, not for simultaneous use together.
- Vanilla Expanded Achievements is no longer included for the 1.6 version.
- The old scenario was removed due to reported issues with loading and map generation.

[b]FAQ[/b]
[b]Is it compatible with CE?[/b]
Mostly. Combat Extended currently ships a built-in patch for this mod. However, I have not fully tested it with changes I made for 1.6 and I'm not sure if everything is patched. Normally, I'll just include the CE patch in here, but since CE itself already ships a patch for the previous version it gets complicated.

[b]Can it be added to an existing save?[/b]
Usually yes.

[b]Can it be removed from a save?[/b]
Like most content mods, removal is risky if existing pawns, items, or defs from the mod are already present.

[b]Banners and shields?[/b]
Banners and shields are implemented in the 1.6 continued version through the Vanilla Expanded Framework compatibility path. You MUST have VEF in your mod list to have banners and shield appear in game.

[b]What happened to the scenerio?[/b]
Removed due to some issues, will add it back later.
